Motion Graphics

Tell your story in an engaging way.

Motion graphics give life to visuals. It uses the fourth dimension-time, to make communication effective, and emphasis valuable points, using possibilities of animation. Beetroot can help you use motion graphics to enhance marketing strategies, communication design, learning design, info graphics, and data visualisations.
Our work for motion graphics comprises script writing, storyboarding, visual design, 3D modelling, 2D,3D animations, online videos, corporate videos, explainer videos, whiteboard animations etc. Expertise in all aspects of movie production-from live recording to post production- is our strength, this help us plan each project accurately and complete on time.

User Experience And Design

There are three levels of experience, visceral, behavioural and reflective.
Usability is one of the fundamental aspects of any product or service. Understanding target user and a human centred approach to design are necessary to create a user friendly product or service. Our experienced Usability Analysts and designers apply various psychological techniques to understand right user needs and find intuitive solution for design problems. We provide UX consulting to understand problems with existing design, create fresh design after UX research. Our process includes, research, ideation, prototype and test.
The process comprises user interviews, contextual inquiry, card sorting and information architecture, persona creation, wire framing, formative testing of prototypes, visual design, usability test of final products. Great UX is beyond usability, we help our customers to 'gamify' applications, to make the user experience more engaging.
